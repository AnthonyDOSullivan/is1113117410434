Amazon

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a secure cloud services platform, offering compute power, database storage, content delivery and other functionality to help businesses scale and grow. AWS allows users to have at their disposal a virtual cluster of computers, available through the Internet. AWS was launched in July 2002 and then re-launched in 2006. AWS's version of virtual computers have most of the attributes of a real computer including hardware, local/RAM memory, hard-disk storage, operating systems, and appplication software. Amazon Web Services are trusted by a wide-range of customers including Spotify and Johnson and Johnson.
